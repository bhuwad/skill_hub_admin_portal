Welcome to SKILLHUB

Software Developer Program for Freshers (INR 1,10,000 + 18% GST) Free!

Welcome to Skill Hub 6-Month Free Software Developer Program for Freshers! We are delighted to introduce this unique initiative designed to provide aspiring software developers like you with a valuable opportunity to kickstart your careers without any financial burden. If you have a passion for coding and a desire to build innovative solutions, this program is the perfect launchpad for your journey into the world of software development.

01

Why Choose us

At Edubin, we believe in the power of technology to drive positive change and transform lives. Our journey began with a vision to bridge the gap between young, aspiring software developers and the opportunities in the IT industry. With the invaluable support of Rotary International, we have embarked on a mission to make a difference.

02

Our Mission

Eduin is a 6-month training and internship program designed to empower software developer freshers from underprivileged backgrounds in rural areas, tier 2, and tier 3 cities. Our curriculum, developed by industry experts, is carefully crafted to equip our students with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the competitive IT landscape.
